# music-store-analysis-sql
SQL-based analysis of a digital music store database exploring customer behavior, sales performance, artist popularity, genre trends, and revenue insights using joins, subqueries, and CTEs.
# 🎵 Music Store Analysis | SQL Project

## Overview

This SQL project analyzes a digital music store database to uncover valuable business insights related to customers, sales, artists, genres, and revenue generation.

Using SQL queries, the project explores customer purchasing behavior, top-performing artists, popular music genres, revenue trends, and geographic sales distribution. The goal is to demonstrate how SQL can be used to answer real-world business questions and support data-driven decision-making.

---

## Business Problem

The music store wants to better understand its customers and sales performance by answering key business questions such as:

- Who are the most valuable customers?
- Which artists generate the most revenue?
- What are the most popular music genres?
- Which countries and cities contribute the most sales?
- How do customer purchasing patterns vary across regions?

This analysis helps identify opportunities for improving marketing strategies, customer engagement, and revenue growth.

---

## Dataset Description

The project uses the Chinook Music Store Database, which contains information about:

- Customers
- Employees
- Invoices
- Invoice Lines
- Artists
- Albums
- Tracks
- Genres
- Playlists

The database simulates a digital music store and provides transactional sales data for analysis.

## Business Questions Solved

### Employee Analysis

- Identify the senior-most employee based on job title.

### Sales Analysis

- Find countries with the highest number of invoices.
- Identify the top invoice values.
- Determine the city generating the highest revenue.

### Customer Analysis

- Identify the best customer based on total spending.
- Analyze customer spending patterns.
- Find customers who listen to Rock music.

### Artist & Genre Analysis

- Identify the top Rock artists.
- Determine the most popular music genres.
- Analyze artist-wise revenue contribution.

### Track Analysis

- Find tracks longer than the average song length.
- Analyze music preferences across customers.

### Revenue Analysis

- Calculate revenue generated by artists.
- Determine top-performing artists.
- Analyze customer spending on specific artists.

## Key Insights

### Customer Insights
- A small group of customers contributes a significant portion of total revenue.
- High-value customers can be targeted for loyalty programs.

### Geographic Insights
- Certain countries and cities generate substantially more revenue than others.
- Regional sales trends can support location-based marketing campaigns.

### Artist Performance
- A few artists contribute the majority of sales.
- Popular artists can be leveraged for promotional activities.

### Genre Analysis
- Rock music dominates customer preferences.
- Understanding genre popularity helps improve content recommendations.
